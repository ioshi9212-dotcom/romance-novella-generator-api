When creating a new NPC, generate a fixed character card and then treat it as canon.

Required fields:
- id
- name: non-Russian, not from the user's private novel
- age
- role
- appearance_locked: true
- appearance: height, build, hair, eyes, face, hands, clothing_style, distinctive marks
- habits: at least 4 concrete habits
- speech_style: tone, sentence length, forbidden speech patterns
- personality: core traits, flaws, conflict style, comfort/inconvenience behavior
- backstory: summary, old connections, secrets
- private_goals
- boundaries: what they will refuse, what they may help with, what triggers anger or fear

NPCs must not exist only for the player. They need their own obligations, relationships, plans, fears, and blind spots.
